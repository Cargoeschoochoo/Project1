Thanasis' Background!

Thanasis Antetokounmpo is a NBA player who is currently playing on the Milwaukee Bucks. He is the eldest brother of the Antetokounmpo family, which includes Giannis Antetokounmpo and Kostas Antetokounmpo. He plays the small/power forward position and currently has a championship ring. He also played in the Euro League for Greece's minor leagues on the team known as the Filathlitikos. He has also played for the Greece national team. He wears the jersey number of 43 as a reference to his brother Giannis' number of 34 but reversed. Thanasis is known as the heart and soul of the team, and his presence alone makes the Milwaukee Bucks play better, especially when it comes to Giannis.
